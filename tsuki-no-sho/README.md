# Tsuki no shō　月之抄

The Tsuki no shō (月之抄) or Reflections on the Moon by the legendary swordsman Yagyū Jūbei Mitsuyoshi is one of the most famous classical works on swordsmanship.

The source text is taken from 史料柳生新陰流（下巻） (April 1995, pp. 9-80), edited by Imamura Yoshio.
